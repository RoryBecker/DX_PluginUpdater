 - The existing system is just a plain list of plugins.
	
	CR_StyleNinja

 - Version 1 feeds will to be simple lists of plugins and assume download location of rorybecker.co.uk

	<Doc>
		<Plugins>
			<Plugin Name="CR_StyleNinja" />
		</Plugins>
	</Doc>

 - Version 2 feeds can override DownloadFolder

	<Doc>
		<Plugins>
			<Plugin Name="CR_StyleNinja" />
			<Plugin Name="My_OtherPlugin" BaseDownload="http://MyLocation/MyOtherPlugin" />
		</Plugins>
	</Doc>

 - Version 3 can use aliases

	<Doc>
		<Aliases>
			<Alias Name="DefaultCommunity" Value="http://www.rorybecker.co.uk/DevExpress/Plugins/Community/" />
		</Aliases>
		<Plugin Name="{DefaultCommunity}CR_StyleNinja" />
		<Plugin Name="My_OtherPlugin" BaseDownload="http://MyLocation/MyOtherPlugin" />
	</Doc>

 - Version 4 will introduce Feed Collections aka Packages

The point of this is so that new feeds can be created and used without new deployments.
